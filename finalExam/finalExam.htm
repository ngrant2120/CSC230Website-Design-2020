About Me

Hello, my name is Nick Grant

And it is my goal to create a professional facing website that can serve to represent the best of that which I have to offer.
I have been exposed to Information Technology elements for as long as I can remember. It wasn't until 2012 however that I made the decision to pursue a devoted career path within the world of IT.
I graduated from ITT Tech in 2014 with an Associates of Science in Network Engineering. Acquiring that degree served as a major stepping stone to getting my foot in the door. After gaining five years of real world technical experience, I decided to re-enroll in school at Sullivan University to acquire a Bachelore's of Science in Information Technology. Currently, I am set to graduate with my BSIT Degree in June of 2020.
